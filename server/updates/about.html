About Us

We are a group of students with the focus to create an enjoyable and educational game for children in Singapore from Primary 1 to Primary 3. This project will focus solely on Mathematics and we have created 3 games with friendly and cute design.

With our first game, Fishing Race that aim to help students with their Algebra Topics.

Our second game, Fish Shapes that aim to help students with their Geometry Topics.

And our third game, Fish Market that aim to help students with their Money Topics.

What We Cover

Fishing Race:

Primary 1:

  • Addition & Subtraction up to 3 digits
  • Adding and subtracting more than two 1 digit numbers

Primary 2:

  • Additional & Subtraction up to 4 digit
  • Multiplication and division up to 40

Primary 3:

  • Additional & Subtraction up to number 4 digit
  • Multiplication and division up to 3 digits

Fish Shapes

Primary 1:

  • Identifying 2D shapes
  • Able to name Triangle, Square, Rectangle, Circle

Primary 2:

  • Identifying 3D shapes
  • Able to name Sphere, Cylinder, Cuboid, Cube

Primary 3:

  • Calculating the perimeter of Rectangle, Square, Rectilinear figure
  • Area of Rectangle, Square

Fish Markets

Primary 1:

  • Counting in cents up to $1
  • Counting money in dollars up to $100

Primary 2:

  • Reading money in decimal notation
  • Solving problems in dollars only or in cents only

Primary 3:

  • Add & Subtract money in decimals
  • Solve money problems
